Jamkhed Bus Stand Information

Your key hub for travel in Ahmednagar district.

Key Information

  • **Address:** Bus Stand Road, Jamkhed, Ahmednagar - 413201
  • **Established:** 1974
  • **Operating Hours:** Open from approximately **5:00 AM to 10:00 PM**.
  • **Main Services:** Extensive network of both **ordinary and semi-luxury buses** operated by MSRTC.
  • **Popular Routes:** Pune, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Nashik, and Ahmednagar.
  • **Amenities:** Recent improvements in infrastructure and facilities.

Services Offered

  • **Bus Timings and Schedules:** Information is available to help you plan your journey effectively.
  • **Route Information:** Detailed information about bus routes and stops is provided to help you navigate with confidence.
  • **Booking Assistance:** The MSRTC and various online platforms offer assistance with ticket reservations.

For further details and bus timings, you can check the official MSRTC website or other online booking platforms.
